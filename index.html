Student-Led Initiative
Vision of Perfection: Youth Voices of SEU, Echoed Globally

We are 国际纵队 (International Brigade), a student practice team from Southeast University. Our project, "至善视界" (Vision of Perfection), tells the SEU story to the world through the eyes of young people.

Through desk research and global-alumni interviews, we explored how SEU's 120-year heritage, world-leading engineering, and international partnerships shape lives across borders. We then turned these insights into a multilingual brand narrative — this brochure, short videos, and social content — designed for international audiences.
